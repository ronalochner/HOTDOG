YOGA ETIQUETTE


Etiquette is not a loud and explicit rule; it is soft and subtle and yet always carries you further when you observe appropriate etiquette in the right setting. Yoga, which everyone can do, is no exception. A tradition as ancient and beautiful as yoga comes with a certain level of implied etiquette.

BE RESPECTFUL


PHONES OFF

BE ON TIME



Remove your shoes before entering the studio. Leave them in a cubby in the studio lobby. This preserves the of the sacredness of the space.


Turn off your cell phone. This enhances your experience and is a polite gesture to your fellow students. This is a sanctuary a place to find peace.


Try your best to be on your mat in the studio five minutes before class starts out of respect for your classmates.
